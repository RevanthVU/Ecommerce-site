Details of the retail Store

An organization is a formal arrangement of roles and ranks put in place to attain some particular objectives.
The arrangement of a retail organization depicts the role of employees and the mode in which the organization operates.
To outline an effective retail organization structure, managers/owners must describe store objectives,
recognize the tasks, categorize the tasks and jobs, and allocate responsibilities and power to different positions,
and define the link between them. Organizations can have flat arrangements or vertical (tall) arrangements.
A flat organization arrangement has only one or two levels of management,
whereas a vertical organization has more than two levels of management.
This unit will discuss forms of retail organization and number of organization levels.
